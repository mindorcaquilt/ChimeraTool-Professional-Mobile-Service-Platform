# Credit System Guide

## How Credits Work

ChimeraTool uses a pay-per-operation model. Credits are purchased in packs and never expire. Each operation consumes a fixed number of credits on success — failed operations are not charged.

## Purchasing Credits

Credits are purchased directly through your ChimeraTool account dashboard. Available packs: 50 / 100 / 250 / 500 / 1000 credits. Volume pricing applies to packs of 250+.

## Free Operations (0 Credits)
- Device scan and identification
- SIM lock status check
- IMEI check
- All diagnostic functions
- EFS backup (read-only)

## Refund Policy

Credits are refunded automatically if an operation fails before modifying the device. Operations that partially complete are reviewed case-by-case through the support portal.
